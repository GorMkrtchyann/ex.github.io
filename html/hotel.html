About

11 floors and 334 beds. In October 1978, the Shirak Hotel in Gyumri was put into operation.
The subject of pride of the people of Gyumri became one of the "victims" of '88. The building was almost completely destroyed by the earthquake. In 2002, it was dismantled to the 4th floor and finished.

And so the Shirak Hotel continued to live in a dilapidated, half-ruined state, living and torturing the people of Gyumri. It became a silent witness to the 1988 catastrophe. Many Gyumri residents experience the cruel moments of the past days when passing by the building.

And the lower part of the building really has something to be proud of: the frescoes of the famous sculptor Frid Soghoyan decorate the wall.
