About

The old white building of the Gyumri Theater, formerly known as the "People's House", is located at the beginning of Gyumri Central Park. This 150-year-old building was built with the investment of the people of Alexandropol. Over time, it has served as a home for people; it has been a major venue for concerts, literary evenings, open discussions, poetry classes, theatrical performances, and other events.

The first opera performed by Armen Tigranyan, "Anush", was first shown here in 1912. 30s of the 20th century. A. The Mravyan Drama Theater was located in the building, then it served as the Builders' Cultural Palace. After the 1988 earthquake, the Shirak TV and Radio Company started working in the former building of the theater.

Today it belongs to the public. Now it has been rebuilt and turned into a Tumo creative technology center.
