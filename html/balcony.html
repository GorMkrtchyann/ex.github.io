About

Many, many phrases in Albert Mkrtchyan's autobiographical film "Tango of Our Childhood" have become commonplace, such as "I lit the moon and I must light it." The film, shot in 1984, is about an Armenian woman in the post-war years, her struggle to preserve her family.

Part of the huge part of the balcony was built in 1895 by Khachatur Abov. As it is mentioned in the inscription on the wall, it belonged to the famous Khoyetsyans. The other section was built in the early 1900s, where the former city post office was located. The structures are very similar, you will not notice with the naked eye that they are built at different times.

Two different earthquakes of 1926 and 1988 devastated the building, despite this, homeless families lived here for a long time. A part of the building was renovated with the financial support of the United Nations Development Program։ Armenia։ the Italian government. In the renovated part, preserving the former appearance of the building, the Shirak regional library has been operating since 2012.
